üí° Remember: Think of each HTML element as a box with 4 layers from outside to inside:
1. Margin ‚Üí Pushes other elements away (invisible space outside)
2. Border ‚Üí The visible edge around your element
3. Padding ‚Üí Creates breathing room inside (space between border and content)
